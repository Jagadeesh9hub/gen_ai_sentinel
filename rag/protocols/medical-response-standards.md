# Medical Response Standards

Targets and practices for emergency medical response.

## Response Time Targets
The target on-scene response time for priority-1 medical calls is 8 minutes; for priority-2, 12 minutes. Sustained averages above target in a district indicate capacity strain and should trigger review and possible pre-positioning.

## Mass-Casualty Indicators
Multiple medical calls clustered in space and time — especially with shared symptoms such as difficulty breathing or dizziness — may indicate a common environmental cause (for example a gas leak) rather than independent events. Treat as a single developing event and coordinate with hazmat.

## Surge Handling
During a surge, hold the highest-priority calls for the nearest available units and pull mutual-aid ambulances early. Re-assess every 15 minutes against the demand forecast.
